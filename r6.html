I designed my three solid color vinal stickers in Corel Draw. I chose three images from online makking sure they had a distinct outline. Once the outline was created on each image I printed them using the Roland GS-24 CAMM 1. I changed the color for each sicker and placed them on my notebook. I am happy with how these resulted and I want to make more of these in the future.

I also designed these two multi colored stickers in Corel Draw. I used a similar process but needed to alter a few setting to fit the printers needs. The Roland 540i printer requires a special shade of pink to be used as an outline of the image so it can detect where to cut out the sicker. I had some trouble figuring out how to outline the image and remembering all of the steps required. I have very satisfied with my sticker, and many people have asked if I could print them some!

Okay Hand Design






























I began this project by using a previously designed object from Thingaverse. I chose to make a mold using the character No Face from one of my favorite movies Spirited Away . The file was imported into Meshmixer, cut it in half and saved as a stl file. Mr. Gerber milled my file over Spring break and as a class we made a silicone mold and poured chocolate to make lolipops.

No Face Design






























I began designing my pewter casting project in Corel Draw. I decided to make a one sided coin based off of the Claddagh ring I wear. Once I found a design I liked I added three trinity knots on the top edge. I also measured the diameter of a dowel rod and inserted four circles on the corners with those dimentions to make the lining up process much easier.
