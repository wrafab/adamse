I designed my three solid color vinal stickers in Corel Draw. I chose three images from online makking sure they had a distinct outline. Once the outline was created on each image I printed them using the Roland GS-24 CAMM 1. I changed the color for each sicker and placed them on my notebook. I am happy with how these resulted and I want to make more of these in the future.

I also designed these two multi colored stickers in Corel Draw. I used a similar process but needed to alter a few setting to fit the printers needs. The Roland 540i printer requires a special shade of pink to be used as an outline of the image so it can detect where to cut out the sicker. I had some trouble figuring out how to outline the image and remembering all of the steps required. I have very satisfied with both of my stickers, especilly the okay hand which many people have asked if I could print them some!
