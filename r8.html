For my first soldering project I made a track light. We recieved a kit containing various parts and pieces that had to be placed a specific spots. We also had to be mindful of the polarity of some of the pieces. We soldered the pieces to the circuit board using a soldering iron that heated up to 750 degrees. My track light functioned when I plugged it into a battery and I am satisfied with my final project.











For my second soldering project I made a sound to light unit. I followed the same process I did for my first project and my unit worked ended up working and I was proud because not many got this project to work.
